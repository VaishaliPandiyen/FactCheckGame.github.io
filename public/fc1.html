Claim 01

Involves...

Robert Moore, a retired university dean who lives in San Jose, California

Background:

To celebrate his 100th birthday, his family invited neighbours to line up with their dogs outside a family home.

Claim

Over 200 dogs showed up and lined up around the block to wish Robert a happy birthday.

View Clues (and sources)

Robert Moore is a dog lover.

The dogs wore their birthday best —
tuxedos and mini cowboy hats — and came with cards, flowers, cupcakes
and, of course, doggy kisses.

A Facebook reel with photos and videos of Moore’s big day
got over 440,000 likes and 90,000 shares

His family posted on the neighbourhood app Nextdoor and Facebook,
asking dog owners to stop by with their canines for pet parade.

Moore’s daughter Alison Moore, told The Washington Post that her father has loved dogs all his life and wants to pet each one he sees.

Politifact says the claim is "Good Enough To Be True"
