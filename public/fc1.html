Claim 01

Involves...

Robert Moore, a retired university dean who lives in San Jose, California

Background:

To celebrate his 100th birthday, his family invited neighbours to line up with their dogs outside a family home.

Claim

Over 200 dogs showed up and lined up around the block to wish Robert a happy birthday.

Clue(s)

Clues

Robert Moore is a dog lover.

A reel that displayed photos and videos of Moore’s big day amassed more than 440,000 likes and was shared more than 90,000 times

The dogs wore their birthday best — which included tuxedos and mini cowboy hats — and came with cards, flowers, cupcakes and, of course, doggy kisses.

The family wanted to do something special to mark his milestone birthday and posted on the neighbourhood app Nextdoor, and on Facebook, asking dog owners in the area to stop by with their canines for a June 17 pet parade.

Moore’s daughter, Alison Moore, told The Washington Post that her father has loved dogs all his life and wants to pet each one he sees.
